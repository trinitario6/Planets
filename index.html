How to Play
🎯 Mission Briefing
Swap adjacent planets to line up 3 or more of the same kind.

Matched planets vanish, the board refills from above, and chain reactions earn bonus points.

Reach the sector score target before your moves run out to advance to the next sector.

Swipe a planet to swap it, or tap two planets in a row to swap them.
🌍 The Planets
8 planets roam the board — match 3 or more of the same type.
✨ Special Objects
Bigger matches create powerful special objects.
⭐🌑
Special + Special
Swap any two specials together
